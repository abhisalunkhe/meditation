What is Meditation??

Meditation can be defined as a set of techniques that are intended to encourage a heightened state of awareness and focussed attention.Meditation is also a sonsciousness changing technique that has been shown to have a wide number of benefits on psychologiacal well-being.


Some key things to note about meditation:

- Meditation has been practiced in cultures all over the world for thousands of years.
- Nearly every religion, including Buddhism, Hindusim, Christianity, Judaism, and Islam has a traditon of using meditative practices.
- While meditation is often used for religious purposes, many people practise it independently of any religious or spiritual beliefs or practices.
- Meditation can also be used as a psychotherapeutic technique.
- There are many different types of meditation.

Why it is IMPORTANT??


Going huge popularity in recent years ,the simple practise of meditation has an abundance of health benifits that can alleviate the stress of modern living and bring some balance back to your world.



Let's face it, todays world is a demanding one. With 24-hour connectivity to work and constant digital overload at home, we're more stressed and under more pressure than even before. As a result, our stress response - one of our innate physological mechanisms that should only be triggered in life-threatening situations - is triggered constantly. Not by lions and tigers, but traffic jams, public speaking, heavy workloads, and financial and relationship troubles.

How does Meditation help?

Thy psychologiacal benifits of meditation are far-reaching and the more you practice, the more benifits you experience.
Benifits are:
* Improved learning ability and memory;
* Improved focus and concentraion;
* Higher confidence in personal ability;
* Increased productivity;
* Enhanced mood and emotional stability;
* Reduced anxiety and stress;
* Increased compassion;
* Greater creativity;
* Reduced anger;
* Reduced of negativity thoughts;
* Increased happiness.

As well as many psychological benifits, there are a surprising number of physical benefits too, such as:
* Decreased physical tension;
* Lower blood pressure;
* Strengthened immune power;
* Slowing the ageing process;
* Higher energy level;
* Improved sleep quality;
* Reduced physical symptoms of stress and anxiety such as digestive problems, sleep, headaches or muscle tension.
